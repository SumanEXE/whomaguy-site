FAQ

The real questions.

What does this actually cost?

Most engagements are between $5,000 and $40,000 depending on scope. Productized offerings have fixed pricing on their pages. We send a real number before you sign anything.

Are you an agency?

No. An agency sells you a roster. Whomaguy is one operator, with a tight network of specialists pulled in when the work calls for it.

How do you handle the marketing-vs.-systems split?

Most engagements touch both. The two wings exist so we can scope clearly, not because the work splits cleanly in practice.

What if I just want a one-off audit?

That's offered as a productized engagement. Fixed price, 10 business days, written deliverable.

Who do you NOT work with?

Pre-revenue founders, agencies hiring us to white-label, and anyone who needs three rounds of approval before a decision gets made.
